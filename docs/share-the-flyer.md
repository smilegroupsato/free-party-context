# Share the Flyer

The flyer is meant to travel.

It does not need a campaign.

It does not need urgency, reach targets, or a complete explanation.

It only needs to reach someone who may notice the atmosphere and wonder what is already forming behind it.

---

## Flyer

```text
https://eternalfree.party
```

The web is the flyer.

The repository is the venue.

---

## A short way to share it

```text
Eternal Free Party

The web is only a flyer.
The repository is the venue.

A place before the village.

https://eternalfree.party
```

## A quieter way

```text
A path, a few lights,
and a place still becoming visible.

https://eternalfree.party
```

## An invitation for another language

```text
If this place begins to speak in your language,
you are welcome to answer in that language.

Not as a literal translation.
As another observation from the same place.

https://eternalfree.party
```

---

Share the flyer as an invitation, not as an instruction.

Do not promise what the place will become.

Do not explain away its ambiguity before the visitor arrives.

Let the flyer carry only enough atmosphere for someone to ask:

> What is happening there now?

The rest belongs to the repository.
